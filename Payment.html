Terms of delivery

After placing an order on the website, our manager will contact you to clarify and confirm the availability of goods in stock.


The delivery time of the order is 2-3 business days following the day of order confirmation. Delivery is carried out on weekdays from 10:00 to 18:00 hours.


Delivery is carried out by courier to the address specified when placing the order. Before departure, the courier will contact you by phone to clarify the convenient time of receipt of the order and delivery details.


The maximum number of products in one order is 3 units.


The cost of delivery is free of charge, if the order amount exceeds 15,000 tenge.


The cost of delivery if the order amount is less than 15,000 tenge is 1000 tenge.


* In some cases, the cost and terms of delivery may differ from the standard ones (high load on weekends or holidays, distance from the city center, sales period, increased loads on the courier service, etc.). In this case, the customer will be informed about the change in the cost of delivery and the application of special conditions when confirming the order by phone. Delivery will be carried out only with the customer's consent to the changed terms of delivery and cost.
